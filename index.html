A common understanding of Greek gods and goddesses is that they are personified ideals or passions. This makes abstract concepts simpler to understand. I rather like this idea, so I included it in the website. Athena was labeled the goddess of wisdom, so having her image is a simple way to "show" wisdom. Calliope, on the other hand, was the goddess of inspiration. In fact, they both are secretly included in the logo. The owl is the symbol of Athena, and the scroll is the symbol of Calliope.
